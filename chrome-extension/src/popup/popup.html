ⓘ How Points Work ▼
🪙
Earning Points
You earn 1 point for every completed trip synced during your weekly earning window. Cancelled trips and trips older than 30 days do not earn points.
📅
Sync Window
Your earning window opens every Monday at 00:00 and closes Wednesday at 23:59 (local time). You can sync trips anytime, but only syncs during the window earn points.
🎁
Redeem Rewards
Spend your points on gift cards from the Rewards tab. Points are valid for 12 months from the date earned.
